Who am I?

I’m Koffi Ismael Ouattara, a Ph.D. candidate in AI Security at Ulm University in collaboration with Huawei Munich. My passion for mathematics and computer science has driven me to explore how we can make AI systems more trustworthy, interpretable, and secure.
My research focuses on dynamic trust assessment in neural networks, leveraging Subjective Logic, machine learning, and cybersecurity principles. I’ve worked with organizations such as Nokia Bell Labs and Mithril Security, contributed to multiple EU Horizon projects, and co-authored peer-reviewed publications and a patent.
I’m deeply motivated by solving complex problems — whether it’s using data to achieve human-surpassing accuracy in AI systems or defending against sophisticated cyber threats. For me, math is the language, computer science is the toolkit, and AI security is the mission.
