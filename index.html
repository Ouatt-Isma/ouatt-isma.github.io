Who am I?

I’m Koffi Ismael Ouattara, a Ph.D. candidate in AI Security at Ulm University in collaboration with Huawei Munich. My passion for mathematics and computer science drives my work on making AI systems more trustworthy, interpretable, and secure.

I am working on dynamic and interpretable Trust Propagation in neural networks using Subjective Logic, machine learning, and cybersecurity principles. I’ve worked with Nokia Bell Labs and Mithril Security, contributed to multiple EU Horizon projects, and co-authored peer-reviewed publications and a patent.

For me, math is the language, computer science is the toolkit, and AI security is the mission.
